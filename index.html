Conway's Game of Life

Rules

  1. Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies, as if by under population.
  2. Any live cell with two or three live neighbors lives on to the next generation.
  3. Any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation.
  4. Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes a live cell, as if by reproduction.

Instructions

To set your initial state just click in the cells you want to be "alive" on the right board, cells "alive" are green color. The click on the "Next" button to see wich cell survive or click "Play" to automatically iterate over the life cycle, if you want to pause the game click the "Stop" button. Also you can clear the boar by clicking the "Clear" button.
